Harrisonburg - Rockingham Historical Society

Below are cemeteries with known Latitude and Longitude locations. We have just begun recording them.

Anyone that wants to let us know this information can email to the address on our home page.

If you know the location of a cemetery, you can use Google Earth, or other similar program to find the Lat/Lon coordinates.

Place the mouse pointer on the spot and the Lat/Lon will show at the bottom of the screen.

We use Degrees/Minutes/Seconds and the measurement must be recorded to the tenth of a second for accuracy.
