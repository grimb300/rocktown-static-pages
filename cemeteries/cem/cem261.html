Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryPort Republic Old Presbyterian Church Cemetery
LocationPort Republic, Rockingham County, Virginia. In Harrisonburg, from the corner of Main Street (Route 11) and Port Republic Road (Route 659), travel east 11.1 miles on Port Republic Road. Turn right onto Route 605 (Main Street). Travel 1/10th mile and you will see Port Republic United Methodist Church on the left side of road and the old cemetery is on the left side of road just past the church.
NotesRev. Snapp is buried inside a fence with the rest of his family and the other two reverends are buried next to them surrounded by another fence. The rest of the graves are scattered and not really in any order. They are just recorded by family.
Survey Date and Recorder11 Nov 2003
Martha Lee & Nettie Lee
