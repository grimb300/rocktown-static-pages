Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryCemetery Larry McNeil Farm now Jack Walker Farm
LocationSouthwest of Port Republic, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Harrisonburg, Virginia, take Route 33 East to Route 276 (Wilderness Road) and turn right. Go several miles to Route 668 (E. Timber Ridge Road) and turn left. Stay on Route 668, crossing over North River and turn left onto Rout 677 (Raines Road) and go about 2 or 3 miles. Cemetery is off to the left and cannot be seen from the road. Was Larry McNeil Farm now Jack Walker Farm
NotesCemetery was not physically visited in 2007 due to location. D.A.R. wrote in the 1960s that the cemetery is located on land that was once owned by Richard Austin and earlier by Casper Mefford.
Survey Date and RecorderAug 2007
Bob Saufley; Bob & Lois Emswiler
