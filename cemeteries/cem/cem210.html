Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryOld Athens Church Cemetery (*)
LocationNortheast of Harrisonburg, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Harrisonburg, Virginia, take Route 11 North and turn right onto Route 721 (Fellowship Road). Route 721 intersects Route 717 (Indian Trail Road) and turn left. Continue on Route 717 and take a right onto Route 811 (Fridley's Gap Road). Go a short distance and you will see, on the right side of the road, the abandoned church structure with cemetery behind it.
NotesThe church and cemetery have been abandoned. The cemetery is overgrown and there is no fence around it. It appears there are many unmarked graves here.
Survey Date and Recorder17 Apr 2003
Bennie Getz, Walter Mason, Bob & Lois Emswiler
