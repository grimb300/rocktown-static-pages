Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryMartz Family Cemetery 1
LocationTenth Legion area, Rockingham County, Virginia. Recorded directions in 1960s by the D.A.R.-Off Route 11 (North Valley Pike); property owned by Lloyd Keene, Formerly owned by Thomas Heitz. On former land of Sabastian Martz. Known as Toll-Gate Farm.
NotesIn 2011 no attempt was made to locate this cemetery. Noted in William Algernon Good's book, 1992, Shadowed by the Massanutten (page 441), he wrote the compiled list of unmarked graves was provided by Myrtle Martz Conquist. She also mentions having found what she believed to be the stones (broken) of Sabastian Martz and his wife Mary Magdaleen, and Peter Martz.
Survey Date and Recorder1960s
