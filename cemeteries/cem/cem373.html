Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeterySpring Creek Negro Cemetery (*)
LocationBriery Branch area, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Harrisonburg, Virginia, take Route 42 South (John Wayland Hwy) and in Dayton turn right onto Route 257 (Ottobine Road which changes name to Briery Branch Road). At Ottobine store, Route 257 turns left (south and now Briery Branch Road.) From the Ottobine Store, go 1.3 miles and turn left onto Route 755 (Lambert Town Road). Go 0.9 miles and cemetery is on the right side of the road just before the M/M Douglas Howdyshell home (yellow).
NotesCemetery is in the process of being restored. There are many graves marked with fieldstones and more with no markings at all.
Survey Date and Recorder08 Jan 2008
Kathy Howdyshell; Bob & Lois Emswiler
