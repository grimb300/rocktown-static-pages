CemeteryHolsinger Family Cemetery
LocationRockingham County, Virginia. Located on Route 801 east of Route 259 and Broadway, Virginia. On the Carlson Holsinger farm on hill east of house.
NotesIn 1967 J. Robert Swank wrote: The plot is in an open field originally covered about 125 x 150 feet. There are many in unmarked grave, including, probably, David Holsinger and Michael Holsinger, father of Peter. The early Holsinger homestead. In 2008 no attempt was made to locate this cemetery.
