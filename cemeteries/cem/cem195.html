CemeteryA. W. Adams Place Cemetery
LocationMelrose area, Rockingham County, Virginia. Was located west of Route 721 (Longs Pump Road), approximately one-half mile west of Route 11 at location of a new house along lane.
NotesNo attempt was made to find this cemetery in 2003. In 1967 J. Robert Swank wrote, "The two graves are enclosed by an iron fence. No evidence of other graves." In 1968 the two Wenger burials were moved to Lindale Mennonite Church Cemetery. All information from Mr. Swank.
