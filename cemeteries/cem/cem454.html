Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeterySandy Bottom Cemetery in National Park
LocationSandy Bottom, Rockingham County, Virginia. Travel east on Route 33 from Elkton, through Swift Run Gap area, till you come to Beldor Road (Route 628). Turn right on Beldor Road and go 6/10 mile and turn left onto Sandy Bottom Road (Route 626). Cemetery is located through Cecil Morris property off Sandy Bottom Road.
NotesCemetery is in wooded area. Many unmarked graves and fieldstones with no inscriptions.
