Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryDriver Family Cemetery
LocationSingers Glen area, Rockingham County, Virginia. From downtown Harrisonburg, Virginia, take Route 42 to Route 763 (Mt. Clinton Pike, Singers Glen Road). Go several miles to Route 777 (Peter Driver Lane) and turn left. Go 2/10 mile and cemetery is on the left side of the road at the top of the hill. It is across the street from a home.
NotesCemetery is overgrown and has a wire fence around it. Remark notes by J. Robert Swank and he noted in 1967 that: "John & Amanda don't have headstones and it is not known of others being buried here."
Survey Date and Recorder06 Nov 2007
Bob & Lois Emswiler
