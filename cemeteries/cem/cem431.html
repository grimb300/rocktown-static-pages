Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryHarshbarger Family Cemetery
LocationHarrisonburg, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Harrisonburg, Virginia, take Route 42 South towards Dayton. Cemetery located on the Charles Wampler farm west of Route 42 on top of hill northwest of office along fence.
NotesIn 1967 J. Robert Swank wrote: There is evidence of ten or more graves marked by field stones. Unfenced in pasture field. From burnt deeds, Rockingham Courthouse we found: Deed Book 7 pg 115, April 11, 1825 Daniel Harshbarger to Henry Rhodes-2 plus acres. Deed Book 33 pg 439 Elizabeth Harshbarger, Catherine Harshbarger, Daniel Miller and wife Rebecca to Harshbarger and Shem Harshbarger-14 acres adj. Blossers, Ann Harshbarger, and Abel and Henry Gile. Deed Book 36 pg 437, Nov 3, 1824 Daniel & Elizabeth Garber to Daniel Harshbarger a parcel of land adj. Peter Blosser. Note in minutes-lost-Daniel Harsbarger from Henry Rhodes. In 2003 checked with Charles and William Wampler and they have no knowledge of this cemetery.
Survey Date and Recorder1967; 2003
J. Robert Swank; Bob & Lois Emswiler
