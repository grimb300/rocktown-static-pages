Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryStrayer Family Cemetery
LocationLynnwood, Rockingham County, Virginia. Take Route 33 East to Route 340 North (East Side Hwy) to Lynnwood. Cemetery is located at Bogota in Lynnwood. Present owners are Mr. & Mrs. Clyde Payne.
NotesCemetery is well maintained and enclosed by iron fence. Evidence in existing trees and stumps show that fence used to cover slightly larger area at one time.
Survey Date and Recorder14 Aug 2008
