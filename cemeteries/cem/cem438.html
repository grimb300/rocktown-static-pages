Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryKeller Family Cemetery
LocationRockingham County, Virginia. Cemetery is located north of Route 769 from spring along line fence between the Miller and Baugher place. On the old Burkholder place now owned by Mrs. Joseph (Evelyn Miller) Wenger and brother Benjamin Miller in Antioch-Amberly section.
NotesIn 1967 J. Robert Swank wrote: Mrs. Hazel Baugher recalls stones for 15 to 20 graves in former years. There is no fence. All stones down in open pasture. In our check of deeds relating to the Miller, family, we found the Kellers mentioned as adjoining property owners.
Survey Date and Recorder1967
J. Robert Swank
