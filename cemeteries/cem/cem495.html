Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryChamp G. Jennings Family Cemetery
LocationMauzy Area, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Harrisonburg, Virginia, take Route 11 north (North Valley Pike) to Route 608 (Mauzy Athlone Road) and turn right. Follow Route 608 and cemetery is on right side. It sits right beside the road.
NotesCemetery is enclosed and over-grown. In 2011 no attempt was made to record this cemetery. In remarks column the notations made "Other record shows" are taken from Marie Koontz Arington's book Mountain Valley People (page 366).
Survey Date and Recorder1960s & 1970
D.A.R. & Marie K. Arington
