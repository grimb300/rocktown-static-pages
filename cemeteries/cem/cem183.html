Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeterySamuel Rhodes Farm Cemetery
LocationBroadway area, Rockingham County, Virginia. Was located east of Route 42 south of Broadway, on Samuel Rhodes farm, now John R. Brenneman.
NotesNo attempt was made to find this cemetery in 2003. In 1967 J. Robert Swank wrote: "This plot is south of the Timothy Showalter home on the hill in open pasture. No evidence of it could be found. Neighbors remember there were a few graves. County Deed records indicate transfer from Daniel Rife and wife, Mary, to David Bare in 1821 from Bare heirs to Samuel Rhodes, to Noah Rhodes. Noah & wife to Benjamin Wine and Berry Wine & wife Gertrude to John R. Brenneman in 1938. Samuel Rhodes died prior to 13 Sep 1857."
Survey Date and Recorder1967
J. Robert Swank
