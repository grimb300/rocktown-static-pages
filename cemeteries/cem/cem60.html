Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryMoffett Family Cemetery
LocationNear Timberville, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Harrisonburg take Route 42 north to Timberville. Stay on Route 42 through Timberville & turn right onto Route 211 (New Market Road). Proceed to Route 953 (Plains Mill Road) and take a left. Go approximately 1.9 miles & cemetery is on the right side of the road. Turn into driveway & get permission at the house to visit the cemetery.
NotesUpon entering cemetery, rows are recorded from front to back and from left to right. It is enclosed by an iron fence & maintained. In 1967 J. Robert Swank noted "The Moffet plantation now owned by Harold Hingerdner. There may be others resting here. Many stones down."
Survey Date and Recorder28 Mar 2002
Bennie Getz, Walter Mason, Bob & Lois Emswiler
