Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryDonovan Cemetery
LocationHopkins Gap area, Rockingham County, Virginia. Cemetery was located about one half mile north of Gospel Hill Church and west of present Route 612 (Hopkins Gap Road).
NotesNo attempt was made to find this cemetery in 2003. In 1967 J. Robert Swank wrote that this "cemetery was destroyed by high water. There were perhaps as many as 25 graves." All data on this cemetery furnished by Mr. Swank.
