Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryHollar Family Cemetery
LocationNorth of Singers Glen area, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Harisonburg, take Route 42 north and turn left onto Route 753 (Wengers Mill Road). Go about 3.3 miles and Route 753 turns right (name changes to Hollar School Road). Continue on Route 753 about 1.1 mile. House on left side of road and cemetery is behind house next to poultry shed. Need permission to drive up to cemetery.
NotesCemetery is well maintained with a fence around it. In 1967 J. Robert Swank wrote "there probably are unmarked graves." Notes in "Remarks" column were made by Mr. Swank.
Survey Date and Recorder4 Apr 2003
Bob & Lois Emswiler
