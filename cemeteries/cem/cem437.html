CemeteryJohn Sellers (Zellers) Cemetery
LocationMauzy area, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Harrisonburg, Virginia, take Route 11 North to Mauzy and cross over I-81, then take a right onto Route 608 (Mauzy Athlone Road). Continue on Route 608 and take a right onto Route 807 (Koontz Corner Road). Go a distance, cemetery is located on left side of the road in an open field with white fence around it and it sits in front of woods. Present owner of the land is Andy Arrington.
NotesWell maintained and has a fence around it. Need permission from the owner to enter this cemetery. In 1960s the D.A.R. wrote: 7 or 9 more graves-limestone and sandstone markers.
