Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryStrickler Family Cemetery
LocationCourt Manor area, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Harrisonburg, Virginia, take Route 11 North (No. Valley Pike) to Route 796 (Moores Mill Road) and turn right. Cemetery is located on the east side of Smith's Creek.
NotesThe cemetery is inside an iron fence next to the Wise Cemetery located on Route 796 (Moores Mill Road). Their ashes remain and rest in this cemetery, however, the stones for these four Stricklers were moved to the Fairview Church of the Brethren some years ago. Only three footstones remain in the Strickler Cemetery.
Survey Date and Recorder1960s & 2008
D.A.R.; Bob & Lois Emswiler
