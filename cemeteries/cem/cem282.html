Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryErnest Dove Cemetery
LocationFulks Run area, Rockingham County, Virginia. Located on Ernest Dove's farm on the Shenandoah Mountain off Little Dry River Road.
NotesCemetery records compiled by Shirley Cullers Miller using headstones, field/river rocks, papers of Warren Souder and Lewis Yankey, J. Robert Swank’s 1967 records, and through interviews with area residents. Only one burial noted by Shirley Cullers Miller. In 1967 J. Robert Swank's note read; "D. Burkholder said the Dove place is on a road left from the store at Criders. Hard surface part way and dirt later. Ernest got in and out from place on horse back."
