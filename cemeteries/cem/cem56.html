Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryAdam M. Long Family Cemetery
LocationNear Melrose, Rockingham County, Virginia. From downtown Harrisonburg, take Route 11 north (No. Main Street, name changes to No. Valley Pike) and go 6.3 miles to Route 721 (Fellowship Road). Turn right and go 9/10ths of a mile, 0.1 mile past the Fellowship U.M. Church, and cemetery is on the right side of the road.
NotesCemetery is overgrown. Starting in the sw corner of the cemetery, rows are from south to north and & from west to east.
Survey Date and Recorder16 Feb 2002
Deb Wake
