Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeterySt. Jacob's Lutheran Church Cemetery
LocationPleasant Valley area, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Harrisonburg, take Route 11 South. Turn left onto Route 679 (Pleasant Valley Road) and go 3.0 miles. The cemetery is on the right side of the road. Located at intersection of Route 679 and Route 689 (Spaders Church Road).
NotesCemetery is well maintained. Rows are recorded starting near Route 679 and going up to the church and headstones are recorded from left to right. Section 001 is the main body of the cemetery and Section 002 is the far right side of cemetery nearest the fence and facing the church.
