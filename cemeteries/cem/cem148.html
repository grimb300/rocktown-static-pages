Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryHoman Family Cemetery
LocationBroadway, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Court Square in Harrisonburg, take Route 42 north about 12.7 miles and turn left onto Route 1414 (Turner Drive). Go 3/10ths mile and turn right onto Skymont Drive. Cemetery is in front yard of the second house on the left of street.
NotesIn 1967 J. Robert Swank noted "The Whites are to be commended for placing a stone wall around the plot and preserving it. It is not known the extent of the original plot. The settlement of the John R. Homan estate in 1883 by Alex Kerr gives the following names: Mary L. Homan, J. H. Riddle & wife, J. H. Jordon & wife, Chas. E. Homan, Frank D. Homan, Wm. D. Homan & G. W. McFarland & wife Dorcas L. Marriage record of John R. Homan to Deborah Riddle (dau. of John Riddle) 13 June 1827, to Margaret Moore (dau. of Charles Moore) 4 Sept 1821, to Mary Dyer (dau. of Margaret Riddle & Wm. Dyer Sr.) 28 Dec 1837."
Survey Date and Recorder17 Oct 2002
Bennie Getz, Walter Mason
