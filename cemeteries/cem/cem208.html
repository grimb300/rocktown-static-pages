Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryKline Family Cemetery 2
LocationBroadway area, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Harrisonburg, take Route 42 north and turn left onto Route 809 (Trissels Road). Go approximately 3/4 mile and cemetery is on a hill on the left side of the road.
NotesThere is a marker where the Klines were buried that reads: "The Kline Grave Stones were moved to the Linville Cemetery in 1972." (Note-This is the Linville Creek Church of the Brethren cemetery.) There are impressions in the ground indicating there were burials in this location.
Survey Date and Recorder4 Apr 2003
Bennie Getz, Walter Mason, Bob & Lois Emswiler
