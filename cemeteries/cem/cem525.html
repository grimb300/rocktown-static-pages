Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryMerica Cemetery
LocationElkton area, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Harrisonburg, Virginia, take Route 33 East to Elkton. Turn north onto Route 340 (East Side Hwy) and go 2.2 miles to Route 607 (Red Brush Road). Turn right onto Route 607 and go 1/2 mile to Route 603 (Blose Road). Turn left onto Route 603 and go 1.6 miles to Route 609 (Naked Creek Road). Turn right on Route 609 and go 1.8 miles to Country Road. Turn right onto Country Road. Cemetery is located about 1/10 mile on right side of road. Cemetery is located right next to road on a high bank.
NotesWell maintained. This is a large area. There are many graves marked with stone rocks and numerous headstones with no writing on them.
Survey Date and Recorder09 July 2011
Bob & Lois Emswiler
