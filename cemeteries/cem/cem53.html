Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryMessick Family Cemetery
LocationWest of Timberville, Rockingham County, Virginia. From downtown Harrisonburg, take Route 42 North. In Broadway, Route 42 turns right and then left. (Names for Route 42 are Harpine Highway, E. Lee St., Timber Way, S. Main St., & N. Main St.) Travel 15.7 miles and turn left onto Route 881 (Orchard Drive). Go 2.3 miles and turn right onto Route 614 (Mechanicsville Road). Go 1.0 mile & you will see a sign "Sonny Side Poultry" on the right side of the road. Turn into drive and cemetery is on the left side.
NotesCemetery is not well maintained & enclosed by a wire fence. In 1967 J. Robert Swank noted "Cemetery is located on the G. W. Cullers farm on the east side of 614, NW of Road 881 & Byrd Storage. There are, perhaps, ten more graves, unmarked. Property formerly Hingardner & Orbaugh place." In 2002 owned by Sonny Side. Comments in the remarks were recorded by Mr. Swank.
Survey Date and Recorder21 Mar 2002
Bennie Getz, Walter Mason, Bob & Lois Emswiler
