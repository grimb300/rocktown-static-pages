Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryKline Family Cemetery 1
LocationBroadway, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Court Square in Harrisonburg, take Route 42 north. Go 11.9 miles and turn left onto Route 1415 (Early Road). Go 3/10ths mile and Route 1415 curves to right but go straight up drive and park by barn. Follow the fence on the north side of the pasture field about 100 yards to the cemetery. The old road between Bowman's Mill and Cedar Run is visible next to this cemetery.
NotesProperty is presently owned by Rev. Don Smith. Need permission to visit cemetery. Cemetery has an iron fence around and is small and overgrown with brush. In 1967 J. Robert Swank noted "There is space for perhaps six additional graves. On Algers Robin Roost Farm."
Survey Date and Recorder17 Oct 2002
