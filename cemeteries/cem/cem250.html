Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeterySamuel Heiston Cemetery
LocationNorthwest of Elkton, Rockingham County, Virginia. From downtown Harrisonburg, Virginia, take Route 33 East towards Elkton. Turn left onto Route 602 (East Point Road) and go about 5.5 miles. Cemetery is on the right side of road hidden by brush. Get permission to visit cemetery from the house nearby.
NotesCemetery is covered with growth and brush. In 1960s the Daughters of the American Revolution recorded this cemetery and noted, "Plot enclosed with an iron fence. Other stones have been removed by vandals. Present owner of property is Howard Heiston Hensley."
Survey Date and Recorder02 Sep 2003
