Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryAthens Cemetery (*)
LocationNortheast of Harrisonburg, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Harrisonburg, Virginia, take Route 11 North and turn right onto Route 721 (Fellowship Road). Route 721 intersects Route 717 (Indian Trail Road) and turn left. Continue on Route 717 and go past Route 811 (Fridley's Gap Road) to 2nd house on right (9302 Indian Trail Road). Cemetery is located in a field a short distance behind their buildings. Also may be referred to as Long's Chapel or Zenda.
NotesCemetery is fenced and maintained. There are many field/limestones in the cemetery without markings on them.
