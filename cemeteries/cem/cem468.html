Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryHalterman-Sirk Family Cemetery
LocationBergton, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Harrisonburg, take Route 42 (Harpine Hwy) north to Broadway Turn left onto Route 259 (Brocks Gap Road) and go towards Bergton. Cemetery is located at 20457 Brocks Gap Road (Route 259), Bergton, on hill over looking Brocks Gap Road beside Luke Lehman home. Cannot be seen from Brocks Gap Road. It is across from the Mennonite Camp. Cemetery gate is locked and key is available from the Lehmans.
NotesCemetery is fenced, mowed and in good condition. Rows are recorded from front to back and left to right. Many fieldstones in cemetery. Mrs. Lehman stated when they moved there 15 years ago, she was told that many of the field stones were babies buried in the cemetery. In 1967 J. Robert Swank noted; There are many other graves marked with field stones. Probably 100 to 150 graves. Notes in Remarks Column were made by J. Robert Swank.
Survey Date and RecorderApr 2009
