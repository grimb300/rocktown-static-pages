CemeteryCaplinger Family Cemetery 2
LocationFulks Run area, Rockingham County, Virginia. Two miles south of Fulks Run near junction of Routes 612 and 815, on the Rev. William Keller place.
NotesIn 1967 J. Robert Swank wrote: Thee are 12 to 18 buried here and unmarked. Information furnished by Nannie Turner Shoemaker (Mrs. Claude E. Shoemaker). Former owners: Turners, Hughie Caplinger, Louis Ritchie. In 2008 no attempt was made to locate this cemetery.
