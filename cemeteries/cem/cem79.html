Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryPirkey Family Cemetery
LocationEast of Harrisonburg, Rockingham County, Virginia. From the corner of Main Street (Route 11) south and Port Republic Road (Route 659), travel east 5.3 miles on Port Republic Road. Cemetery is located on the SW corner of Route 659 (Port Republic Road) and Route 276 (Cross Keys Road).
NotesWell maintained. Notes in remarks column were furnished by Deb Wake. In 1931 John W. Wayland noted "In early days the store of David Kyle was located at the intersection of the roads, at or near the site now occupied by the Pirkey graveyard."
