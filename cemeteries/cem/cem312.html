Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryShaver Family Cemetery 2
LocationCedar Run area, Rockingham County, Virginia. From downtown Harrisonburg, Virginia, take Route 42 (Harpine Highway) north. Go 9.9 miles and turn left onto Route 809 (Trissels Road). Go 1.2 miles and turn right onto Route 617 (Sunset Drive). Go 1.6 miles and turn left onto Route 786 (Cedar Run Trail). Go 5/10ths mile and cemetery is on the left side of road in a field.
NotesThe plot is enclosed by an iron fence. There are perhaps a dozen other unmarked graves.The property is owned by Mark Showalter.
Survey Date and Recorder10 Jan 2005
Bennie Getz, Walter Mason, John & Helen Getz
