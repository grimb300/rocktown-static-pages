Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryFoltz Family Cemetery
LocationMt. Valley, Rockingham County, Virginia. Near Mt. Valley, one-half mile off North side of Route 608 on the Mrs. Russell (Ruth) Phillips' farm.
NotesIn the 1960s the DAR made a notation that there were "Seven other graves with limestone markers and no inscriptions". In 1982 Mary Marie Koontz Arrington's book "Mountain Valley People" she wrote "There were possibly graves with limestone markers. There are stones to the Foltzes at Fairview near New Market. They may have been moved and the stones left behind".
Survey Date and Recorder1979
