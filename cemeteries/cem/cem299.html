Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryMay Cemetery (1)
LocationCriders Area, Rockingham County, Virginia. Located on the farm of the George May I, and on Mays Creek Lane at Bergton, Virginia.
NotesCemetery records compiled by Shirley Cullers Miller using headstones, field/river rocks, papers of Warren Souder and Lewis Yankey, J. Robert Swank’s 1967 records, and through interviews with area residents. Located on the farm of the first May settler, George May I. This cemetery is the oldest of 2 cemeteries on this land.
