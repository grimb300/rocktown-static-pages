Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryBaugher Family Cemetery
LocationSandy Bottom, Rockingham County, Virginia. Travel east on Route 33 from Elkton, through Swift Run Gap area, till you come to Beldor Road (Road 628). Turn right on Beldor Road and go 6/10 mile and turn left onto Sandy Bottom Road (Route 626). Continue on 1.4 miles and you will see a dirt/gravel lane on right side of the road. Park and walk up the lane a short distance and you will see cemetery on the left. Present owner of land is Gary Baugher.
NotesCemetery is well maintained.
Survey Date and Recorder05 May 2002
Barbara & Graham Hensley and Lois & Bob Emswiler
