Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryCooper-May Cemetery
LocationBergton Area, Rockingham County, Virginia. Cemetery is located on Crab Run Road (Route 820) near Bergton, Virginia.
NotesShirley Cullers Miller noted: Tradition says there were "old" Coopers buried here before May. Some people also call this the Whetzel Cemetery. Cemetery records compiled by Shirley Cullers Miller using headstones, field/river rocks, papers of Warren Souder and Lewis Yankey, J. Robert Swank’s 1967 records, and through interviews with area residents.
