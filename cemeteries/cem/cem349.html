CemeteryMt. Grove Community Chapel Cemetery
LocationPenn Laird, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Court Square in Harrisonburg, Virginia, take Route 33 East. Drive 7.3 miles and turn left onto Route 842 (Mt. Grove Road). Go 4/10ths mile and cemetery is located behind the church on the left side of the road. Note: The church is now named-Truth, Light and Life Mission Fellowship.
NotesWire fence around cemetery and well maintained.
Survey Date and Recorder23 May 2007
