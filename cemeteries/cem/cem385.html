Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryFulk-Fawley Cemetery
LocationFulks Run area, Rockingham County, Virginia. From the intersection of Route 42 and Route 33 in Harrisonburg, Virginia, take Route 42 North. Go 13.1 miles (which puts you in the town of Broadway) and turn left onto Route 259 (Brocks Gap Road). The cemetery is on the right side of Route 259 about 100 yards before Route 938 (Fawley Lane). It is almost diagonally across Route 259 from Turner Ham Store.
NotesPoorly maintained. Wire fence around cemetery is leaning down in places. Cemetery is larger than the area that is fenced. In 1967 Mr. J. Robert Swank noted; There are many graves with no inscription. Notes in Remarks column were furnished by Mr. J. Robert Swank and Patricia Turner Ritchie.
Survey Date and Recorder12 Jan 2008
Bob & Lois Emswiler
