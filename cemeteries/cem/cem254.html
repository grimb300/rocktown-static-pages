Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryFridley Family Cemetery
LocationLinville, Rockingham County, Virginia. On west side of Route 724 (Melrose Road) about one mile east of Linville on the Orville See Place.
NotesNo attempt was made to find this cemetery in 2003 as it has been plowed over for years. J. Robert Swank in 1967 noted "No markers remain to the six or more graves here." Mr. Swank went on to list some of the Fridley deaths that might possibly be buried in this cemetery. He also listed record from the Courthouse as follows: "Deed Book 24, Page 533 - 15 May 1884 heirs of Charles H. Fridley dec'd: Widow Abigail, Charles H., Benjamin F., and Jane Ann wife of John Delaughter."
Survey Date and Recorder1967
J. Robert Swank
