Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryBrown Family Cemetery
LocationMountain Valley area, Rockingham County, Virginia. Recorded directions in 1960s by the D.A.R.-Located on Route 620 (Mountain Valley Road) near Mt. Valley United Methodist Church on the former Robert Brown Farm, now owned by Mrs. Malcolm Phillips.
NotesIn 2011 no attempt was made to locate this cemetery. In 1960s D.A.R. noted four graves marked with limestone markers, no inscriptions. Large Boxwoods mark site of the graveyard plot. In Marie Koontz Arrington book Mountain Valley People she wrote; This is an old cemetery on James Thompson Jr., farm. The cemetery was cleared and marked with iron pins in each corner by him and is being farmed over.
Survey Date and Recorder1960s
