Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryHuffman - Flick Cemetery
LocationLinville area, Rockingham County, Virginia. From downtown Harrisonburg, take Route 11 north and turn left onto Route 765 (Gravels Road). Cemetery is located on north side of Route 765 approximately one fourth mile east of Bethel Church of the Brethren. The cemetery is located in a field on the Jack Wilmer farm about 250 yards East-North East of the Bolton home. GPS coordinates N 38* 30.028' W 78* 49.628'
NotesIn 1967 J. Robert Swank noted "There are probably 25 to 30 unmarked graves." Notes in "Remarks" column are provided by Wayne Loker.
Survey Date and Recorder25 Nov 2002
Wayne Loker, JoEllen Emswiler, Judy Campbell
