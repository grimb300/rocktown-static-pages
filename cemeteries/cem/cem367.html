Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryKeezletown Alms Cemetery
LocationKeezletown, Rockingham County, Virginia. No longer visible.
NotesThe Keezletown Alms house was torn down before 1900. It was located just off the Keezletown Road on what is now a part of one of the Strickler farms. The cemetery was located in a field that is plowed over and no longer recognizable. It was in existence from about 1800 to 1870. (Information provided by Rev. Bill Zirk.)
