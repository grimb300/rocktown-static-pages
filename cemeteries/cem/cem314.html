Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryWebster Family Cemetery
LocationHarrisonburg, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Harrisonburg, take Route 33 East to Chestnut Ridge and turn right. Go 1/10 mile and turn right on to Blue Stone Hills Drive. Go 5/10 miles and turn left on to Pearl Lane. Go 3/10 mile and cemetery is located downhill, behind home at 2217 Pearl Lane.
NotesProperty where this cemetery is located is now owned by Oak Crest Builders. They are clearing the land around the cemetery. They plan to erect a fence around the cemetery and leave it alone (not moving burials). The Marvin Baker family, who live at 2235 Reservoir Street, is related to the Websters and will be maintaining the cemetery. Cemetery not previously recorded.
Survey Date and Recorder20 Mar 2005
Glenn Huffman
