CemeteryBethel Church of the Brethren Cemetery
LocationMayland, Rockingham County, Virginia. From downtown Harrisonburg take Route 11 North (north Main Street name changes into North Valley Pike). Go 11 miles and turn left on 259 West (Mayland Road). Go 2.3 miles to Route 619 (Phillips Store Road) and turn left. Go 0.2 mile and cemetery is on right side of road.
NotesCemetery rows are from left to right, front to back.
