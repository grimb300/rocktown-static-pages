LocationNortheast of Spring Creek area, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Harrisonburg, Virginia, take Route 42 South (John Wayland Hwy) to Dayton. Go right on Route 257 (Ottobine Road), then go 4.0 miles and turn left onto Road 752 (Silver Creek Road). (Note: Route 752 turns to the right and becomes Beaver Creek Road).
