Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryYancey Family Cemetery
LocationElkton area, Rockingham County, Virginia. Take Route 33 East to town of Elkton, then turn right onto Route 340 south (East Side Highway) to Route 642 (Captain Yancey Road). Cemetery is located on the Bernie Yancey farm at 3829 Captain Yancey Road. You will see HILLTOP sign at road; at top of driveway, cut through field on right.
NotesWell maintained and enclosed by iron fence. The farm is on the Shenandoah River.
Survey Date and Recorder14 Aug 2008
