Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryReedy Cemetery 1
LocationGenoa, Rockingham County, Virginia. South of Route 612 approximately 1/2 mile on Reedy Run, near Oak Grove Brethren Church. Land owned by Ray & Genevea Reedy Smith, descendants of the Reedy Family.
NotesIn 1999 Patricia Turner Ritchie noted: There are many marked graves but only three carved stones. In 1967 J. Robert Swank wrote: 40 to 50 are said to be buried here-only three marked. Overgrown, no fence.
Survey Date and Recorder1999
Pat Turner Ritchie
