Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryStoner Family Cemetery
LocationWest of Timberville, Rockingham County, Virginia. From downtown Harrisonburg, take Route 42 North. In Broadway, Route 42 turns right and then left. (Names for Route 42 are Harpine Highway, E. Lee St., Timber Way, S. Main St., & N. Main St.) Travel 15.7 miles and turn left onto Route 881 (Orchard Drive). Go 2.4 miles and you will see the old entrance on the left side of the road to an inactive zinc mine. The cemetery is up the hill behind the buildings off to the right side.
NotesThe plot is overgrown and is enclosed by a stone wall which is starting to deteriorate. Need to walk through briers & brush to get to the cemetery. Need to obtain permission from the property owner before visiting this cemetery. In 1967 J. Robert Swank noted "There are a number of other, unmarked graves."
Survey Date and Recorder21 Mar 2002
Bennie Getz, Walter Mason, Bob & Lois Emswiler
