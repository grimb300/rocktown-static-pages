Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryKennedy Family Cemetery
LocationHarrisonburg, Rockingham County, Virginia. From downtown Harrisonburg, travel south on Route 11 (Main Street) to Stone Spring Road and turn left. (Note: if you turn right the road is called Pleasant Hill Road.) You will cross over railroad tracks and pass Valley Blox. Go approximately 5/10 miles and you will see the Rockingham Memorial Hospital Wellness Center on the right side of the road before you cross over I-81. Turn right into the Wellness Center and follow the one-way drive through the parking lot. Just before leaving the area, you will see the cemetery, with wire-fence around it, in the corner near the driveway and Stone Spring Road.
NotesCemetery is well maintained.
