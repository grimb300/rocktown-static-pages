Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryMt. Pleasant Mennonite Church Cemetery
LocationDayton, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Harrisonburg take Route 42 South to Dayton. In Dayton turn left onto Route 712 (Mosby Road). Go 6/10th mile and turn right on to Route 701 (Pike Church Road). Go 5/10th mile and turn right (where the church sign is located by a house) and go 1/10th mile. Church and cemetery are on the right side of lane.
NotesCemetery established since previous records in 1960 and well maintained.
