Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryJesse Wyant Family Cemetery
LocationBeldor Hollow, Rockingham County, Virginia. Take Route 33 east to Route 628 (Beldor Road) and turn right. Near head of Beldor Hollow on Chester Heatwole's farm (was George Herring's farm) on the left side of road. It is not in the Shenandoah National Park.
NotesIt is fenced and overgrown. "Remarks" column notes were provided by Lois Bowman in 1987. DAR records of 1960s showed an A.D. and W.B. as being buried in this cemetery. This is part of E. W. information. W.B. means 'was born' and A.D. means 'and died'.
Survey Date and Recorder28 Sep 1987
Lois B. Bowman
