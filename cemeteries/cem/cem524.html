Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryZetty-Carpenter Family Cemetery
LocationNorth Rockingham County, Virginia. From Harrisonburg, Virginia, go north on Route 11 (Valley Pike) to Route 721 (Fellowship Road). Take a right on Route 721 to Route 717 (Indian Trail Road). Go left on Route 717. Bear left at intersection of Route 717 and Route 811 (Fridleys Gap Road). Go about 1000 feet and take first left lane up steep hill. Enter through gate and proceed about 200 feet. Cemetery is all but indistinguishable in a former corner of a field. Must get permission from owner, Buddy Showalter, before visiting cemetery.
NotesNot in good condition. Cemetery is barely recognizable.
Survey Date and Recorder2009
