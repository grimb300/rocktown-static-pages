Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryFunkhouser Family Cemetery
LocationRockingham County, Virginia. Cemetery is located 8.7 miles on the south side of Route 33 West and west of Route 613 on Harry Shifflet farm. Up hillside behind current address 8455 Rawley Pike Road.
NotesIn 1967 J. Robert Swank wrote: There are other graves indicated by field stones. Cemetery surrounded by iron fence. Adjoins the Joseph Crawford Farm. In 2008, several attempts were made to access this cemetery but it is overgrown.
