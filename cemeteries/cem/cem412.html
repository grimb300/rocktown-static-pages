Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeterySummers Family Cemetery
LocationLacey Spring area, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Harrisonburg, Virginia, take Route 11 North (No. Valley Pike) and turn left onto Route 806 (Lacey Spring Road). About 1/4 mile past the Lacey Spring Cemetery on the right side of Route 806.
NotesIn 2008 property owner Mark Arbogast advised us there are no stones for burials on the property. In 1967 J. Robert Swank wrote: All stones have been removed but the three Summers stones were found on the place. It is not known how many were buried here but the deeds included a graveyard of .109 acre. The land was conveyed in 1899 by Solomon Summers to E.D. Davis, by Davis to D.E. Layman 1903, by Layman to Dr. Dyerly, and by Dyerly to Strawderman. Summers names found in deeds were Abraham, Michael, John, Solomon K.
Survey Date and Recorder25 Jan 2008
Mark Arbogast; Bob & Lois Emswiler
