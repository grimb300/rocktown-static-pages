CemeteryHerwin Chapel E.U.B. Church Cemetery
LocationSimmers Valley, Rockingham County, Virginia. Located on Simmers Valley Road (Route 619) near the junction of Longs Pump Road (Route721), N.W. of Linville, Virginia.
NotesFrom the cemetery driveway, rows are from the back (top of hill) to the front and rows run from the driveway to the other side. The church was founded in 1899 and merged with Mt. Tabor Church. The cemetery remains and is maintained.
