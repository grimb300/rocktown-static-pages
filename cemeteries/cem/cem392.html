Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryRhodes (Roth) Family Cemetery
LocationBroadway area, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Harrisonburg, Virginia, take Route 42 (Harpine Highway) towards Broadway. Travel several miles and just before Route 809 (Trissels Road) on the left side of Route 42, is a large brick house and farm (Joseph Shank Farm). The cemetery is located in a field behind the house and chicken sheds. You need permission from Mr. Shank to visit the cemetery.
NotesCemetery is enclosed with wire fence and somewhat maintained. Numerous illegible stones and unknown markers. Notes in the Remarks Column were furnished by J. Robert Swank in 1967. Mr. Swank also indicated there was space for a number of other graves.
Survey Date and Recorder8 Nov 2007
Bob & Lois Emswiler
