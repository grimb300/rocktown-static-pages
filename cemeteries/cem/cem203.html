CemeteryGreenmount Brethren Church Cemetery
LocationGreenmount area, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Harrisonburg, Virginia, take Route 42 North to Route 772 (Greenmount road) and turn left. Stay on Route 772, pass the Greenmount Brethren Church on the left, and cemetery is located on the right side of the road.
NotesSection 001 is the newer part of the cemetery located closest to the road. Section 002 is the older part of the cemetery and is located on the backside of the cemetery. In Section 002 Row 011A and 011B are at the end of Row 011 but going in a different direction. Cemetery rows are recorded from left to right (west to east). Facing headstones they are recorded from left to right (south to north). Notes in the Remarks column were provided by J. Robert Swank.
