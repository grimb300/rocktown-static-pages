Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryGeil Family Cemetery
LocationBroadway, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Court Square in Harrisonburg, take Route 42 north about 12.7 miles and turn left onto Route 1414 (Turner Avenue). Go to South Sunset Drive and turn left. Go 4/10ths mile and turn left onto dirt lane. Drive past poultry house and park by barn. Walk through pasture field to location behind barn.
NotesCemetery also referred to as the Old Bowman Graveyard. Property presently owned by J. Alvin Byers. In 2002 the only marker belongs to Jacob Geil. We did find a couple of footstones & half of an old sandstone marker with no writing on it among a pile of tires. In 1954 Rev. C. E. Nair wrote "The old Bowman graveyard at the Samuel Bowman Mill place where Jacob Geil, Germany immigrant, was buried." In 1967 J. Robert Swank noted "None of the original markers remain. A monument to Jacob Geil has been placed. The plot is in open field. There were others buried here."
Survey Date and Recorder17 Oct 2002
Bennie Getz, Walter Mason
