CemeteryTrout Family Cemetery
LocationEdom area, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Harrisonburg, take Route 42 north and turn left onto Route 809 (Trissels Road). Go approximately 3/4 mile and cemetery is on a hill on the left side of the road.
