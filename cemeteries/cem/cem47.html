CemeteryClover Hill United Methodist Church Cemetery
LocationClover Hill, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Harrisonburg at the corner of High Street and Route 33 West (Rawley Pike) go west 7.5 miles on Route 33 (Rawley Pike). Then turn left onto Route 613 (Clover Hill Road). Go 2.6 miles and turn right onto Fulton School Road. Go 0.2 mile and cemetery is on the right side of the road.
NotesCemetery is well maintained. Rows start at the back (farthest from the church) to the front, and from right to left facing the headstones.
