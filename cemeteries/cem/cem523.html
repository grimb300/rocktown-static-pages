Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryZetty (Zettee) Family Cemetery
LocationTenth Legion, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Harrisonburg, Virginia, take Route 11 North (North Valley Pike) to Route 798 (Arkton Road) at Bethlehem Church. Turn right onto Route 798 and cross over Smith Creek and then War Branch Creek. Go to 4th house on right. Cemetery is about 300 yards on hill behind barn.
NotesIn poor condition. Most of the stones are broken off bases but after removing dirt from their faces, they can be read. In the 1960s the D.A.R. records show - there are other graves where no markings cam be read. Many are rough fieldstones.
Survey Date and Recorder5 Mar 2011
Gene Holsinger
