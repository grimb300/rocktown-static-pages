Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryHitt Family Cemetery
LocationElkton area, Rockingham County, Virginia. Recorded directions in 1960 by the D.A.R.-Cemetery is located on top of hill north on Wolf Run and approximately 1/2 mile east of the Hensley Family Cemetery north of Route 623 (Mt. Pleasant Road). Land owned by Russell Monger.
NotesIn 2011 no attempt was made to locate this cemetery. In the 1960s the D.A.R. wrote; There are no dates and stones are unidentified in many cases. The recorded list is known to have been buried in this plot.
