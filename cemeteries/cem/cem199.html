Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryBowman Family Cemetery 2
LocationWar Branch/Peaked Mountain area, Rockingham County, Virginia. Take Route 33 west to Route 613 (Whitmore Shop Road). Turn right onto Route 613 and go 1.2 mile to Route 726 (War Branch Road). Turn left onto Route 726 and go 6/10 mile. Turn left up private road and go 5/10 mile. Cemetery is to the left of road up in the woods area.
NotesIn 2003 no attempt was made to walk the cemetery. In 1967 J. Robert Swank noted "There are many unmarked graves. The fence is down and livestock have knocked stones over. In 1967 farm was owned by Wayne Driver and previous owners were Showalter-Crawford."
Survey Date and Recorder1967
J. Robert Swank
