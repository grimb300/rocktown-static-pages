Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryMt. Olive Brethren Church Cemetery
LocationPineville area, Rockingham County, Virginia. In Harrisonburg, at the corner of Route 11 South (South Main Street) and Route 659 (Port Republic Road), turn left onto Route 659. Go 8.9 miles and turn left onto Route 672 (Pineville Road). Go 1.7 miles and church/cemetery are on the right side of road.
NotesCemetery is well maintained. Rows are recorded starting at the road and going to the back (behind church). Headstones are recorded starting near the church, working from left to right.
