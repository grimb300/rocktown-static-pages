Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryNew Hope Baptist Church Cemetery (*)
LocationMcGaheysville, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Harrisonburg, take Route 33 East to Route 998 (Old McGaheysville Road) and turn right, then turn right on Dave Berry Road. Go about 1 mile and turn left on a gravel lane. The cemetery is on the left side of the roadway.
NotesCemetery is overgrown. Records show this is a colored cemetery.
