Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryPeak Pentecostal Church Cemetery (*)
LocationWest Rockingham County, Virginia. From the Courthouse downtown Harrisonburg, Virginia, take Route 33 West. Go about 7.8 miles and turn right onto Route 613 (Whitmore Shop Road). Go 6/10ths mile and turn left onto Route 726 (War Branch Road). Go 1.2 miles and church/cemetery on left side of the road.
NotesIn 1967, J. Robert Swank noted, "There are many more unmarked than those with markers. Grounds overgrown and unkept. White Oak Grove Church in 1872." In 2003 the cemetery is maintained but does have some brush around some of the stones.
Survey Date and Recorder03 Oct 2003
