Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryCyrus Rhodes Family Cemetery
LocationMt. Valley area, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Court Square in Harrisonburg, Virginia, take Route 11 North (No. Valley Pike) for 7.1 miles and turn right onto Route 721 (Fellowship Road). Go 1.2 miles and turn left onto Route 717 (Indian Trail Road). Go 3/10ths mile and turn right onto Route 811 (Fridleys Gap Road). Go 1.4 miles to 2525 Fridleys Gap Road and go left into driveway to older brick house presently owned by Mr. & Mrs. Marvin Ogburn. Ask permission to visit cemetery as it is behind the house.
NotesCemetery fairly well-maintained and has brick wall around it. The property was originally owned by Cyrus Rhodes.
Survey Date and Recorder20 Apr 2006
Marvin Ogburn, Bob & Lois Emswiler
