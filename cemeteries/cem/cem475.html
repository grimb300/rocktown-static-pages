CemeteryJollett Cemetery
LocationJollett, Page County, Virginia. Take Route 33 East to Elkton, Virginia. In Elkton take Route 340 North . Go 4.4 miles and turn right onto Route 609 (Naked Creek Road). Go 6.3 miles to Route 759 (Jollett Road). Cross Naked Creek and turn right onto Route 759 and go 2/10 mile. Cemetery is on hill on the left side of the road. There is a lane up to the cemetery.
NotesWell maintained and has a fence around it.
