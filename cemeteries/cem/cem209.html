Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryNave Family Cemetery
LocationCootes Store area, Rockingham County, Virginia. Cemetery was located on the north side of Route 259 (Brocks Gap Road) on hill west of Route 613 (North Mountain Road).
NotesThe cemetery is no longer in existence in 2003. In 1967 J. Robert Swank noted "Cemetery on the old Wander or Estep place. Stones are down and in open pasture field. Some of the family used the spelling Neff. Some think there may have been Fords and Tusings also buried here. Some Naves listed in marriage register: Adam, George, Michael, Abraham, Levi Elias, Washington, Joseph, Elizabeth, Mary, Dorcas, Abby, Cathy, Susan, Sarah, Mary, Rebecca."
