Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryLamon Family Cemetery
LocationMayland area, Rockingham County, Virginia. From downtown Harrisonburg, Virginia, take Route 11 North (No. Valley Pike). Go 9.5 miles and turn left onto Route 809 (Old Sawmill Road). Go 1.5 miles and cemetery is close to the road on the left side.
NotesCemetery is enclosed by a stone wall and being maintained. In 1967 J. Robert Swank noted, "There may be unmarked graves."
