CemeteryRest Haven Cemetery
LocationHarrisonburg, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Court Square in Harrisonburg, Virginia, take Route 11 North 2.1 miles. Cemetery is on the left side of the road.
NotesThis cemetery has been divided into six sections. From the entrances & to the left (south) - Section 1 is closest to Route 11 & Section 2 is directly behind Section 1. Section 3 is the front middle section & Section 4 is directly behind Section 3. Section 5 (children section) is to the right (north) of the entrances & closest to Route 11. Section 6 is directly back from Section 5. Section 1, 2, 3 & 4 stones are recorded from left to right (east to west) & rows are from north to south. Section 5 & 6 stones are recorded from west to east & rows from south to north. Note: There are many graves with headstones & funeral home markers which are recorded in the "Remarks" column. Notes in "Remarks" column provided by J. Robert Swank in 1967.
