Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeterySaufley Cemetery
LocationSouth of Mt. Crawford, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Harrisonburg, Virginia, take Route 11 South. Go several miles and turn left onto Route 682 (Frieden's Church Road). Stay on Route 682 till it dead ends into Route 276 (Cross Keys Road) and turn right. Continue on Route 276 till you can take a left onto Route 678 (Scotts Ford Road). Go about a 1/4 of mile and "Long Meadow Farms" is on the left side of road. Cemetery is in field.
NotesCemetery is maintained by owner, Al Saufley. In the 1960s the D.A.R. noted "two (old) stones were found but there was space for three graves between these stones."
Survey Date and Recorder29 Oct 2003
Al Saufley
