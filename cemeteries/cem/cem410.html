CemeteryMorris Cemetery (#)
LocationGrottoes area, Rockingham County, Virginia. Located on Route 340 (East Side Highway) to Route 659 (Port Republic Road) by Grace Memorial Church. (Most of people spoken to in 2009 did not remember graveyard at intersection. However one elderly man did. He pointed to the field and said that it was there originally but there is no sign of it now.)
NotesNo attempt was made to find this cemetery in 2008. In the 1960s D.A.R. wrote: Graveyard on the old Richard Morris homestead, now vacant. About 25 graves marked with stones. No tombstones with names or dates.
