CemeteryRosenberger - Sipe Family Cemetery
LocationTenth Legion, Rockingham County, Virginia. East of Tenth Legion located off Route 620 on a high hill on the Benjamin White farm.
NotesThis cemetery was recorded in 1979 by Mary Marie Koontz Arrington and published in her book "Mountain Valley People" in 1982. There are several other graves with fieldstone as markers, no writing on them.
