Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeterySmith Cemetery aka Smith Chapel Site
LocationElkton area, Rockingham County and Page County line, Virginia. It is located in Crow Hollow on the Rockingham County and Page County line NE of Elkton, Virginia.
NotesNot well-maintained. William Smith was a part-time preacher, cooper and farmer who deeded a portion of his property for Smith's Chapel and a cemetery. The Chapel was destroyed in the flood of 1870 but the cemetery remains.
