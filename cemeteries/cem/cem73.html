Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryMabel Memorial Presbyterian Chapel Cemetery
LocationHarrisonburg, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Court Square in Harrisonburg, travel east on Route 33 (Market Street). Go 4/10 mile and turn right onto Reservoir Street (just past Woodbine Cemetery on right side of Rt. 33). Stay on Reservoir Street and go 1.7 miles. You will see church/cemetery on the left side of the road.
NotesFacing the cemetery from Reservoir Street, Row 1, Grave 1 is the farthest corner (at back) from the church. The rows are very wavy. Cemetery is well maintained.
Survey Date and RecorderMar 2002
