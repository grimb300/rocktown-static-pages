Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryCrab Run Memorial Cemetery
LocationBergton Area, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Harrisonburg, Virginia, go through Broadway and take Route 259 west (Brocks Gap Road). Drive past Fulks Run Grocery Store and continue on Rt. 259 till you come to Route 820 (Bergton Road) and turn left. Go about 6 miles on Route 820 (name changed to Crab Run Road) and cemetery is on the left side of road. The cemetery is located just beyond the Virginia/West Virginia line.
NotesWell-maintained and is fenced.
Survey Date and Recorder2007
