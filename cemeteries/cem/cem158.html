Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryZion Mennonite Church Cemetery
LocationDahpna, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Court Square in Harrisonburg, take Route 42 north. Go 10 miles & turn right onto Route 809 (Zion Church Road). Go 1.2 miles & church/cemetery are on the left at the junction of Route 809 & Route 803 (Daphna Road).
NotesIn 1967 J. R. Swank noted "Cemetery well kept but many unmarked graves." Notes in "Remarks" column were provided by Mr. Swank. Rows are recorded from left to right (north to south) and headstones from left to right (east to west).
