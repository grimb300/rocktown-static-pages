CemeteryNewtown Cemetery (*)
LocationHarrisonburg, Rockingham County, Virginia. Cemetery is located on Kelley Street, between Sterling Street and Hill Street, in Harrisonburg, Virginia.
NotesWell maintained. There are 4 Sections in cemetery and all sections are recorded, facing headstones, from right to left (west to east). Section 1 is the oldest and has very few headstones standing. Starting at the drive in cemetery and going towards Sterling Street. Section 2 starts across drive from Section 1 and is recorded going towards Sterling Street. Section 3 starts at Hill Street, at the cemetery driveway, and goes towards Section 2. Section 4 starts at the northwest corner of Kelley and Hill Streets and goes towards Section 1.
