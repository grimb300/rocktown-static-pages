Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryUrner Family Cemetery
LocationBroadway area, Rockingham County, Virginia. Was located west of Route 752 near Trissels Mennonite Church on the Andrew Fitzwater place.
NotesNo attempt was made to find this cemetery in 2003. In 1967 J. Robert Swank wrote: "No evidence of this plot, which is on 18 acres of land, sold by Elder John Kline in 1858 to Peter Strine, who later sold to Jacob Singer, remains. From real estate transactions found were: 1811 Jacob & Christian Urner to Daniel Falls; 1834 Martin Urner to David Bowman; Jacob Urner from P. Harlin; Martin Urner from John O'Roark."
Survey Date and Recorder1967
J. Robert Swank
