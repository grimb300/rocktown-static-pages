Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryBrock Cemetery Lacey Spring
LocationLacey Spring, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Harrisonburg, Virginia, take Route 11 North to Lacey Spring, then turn right (east) onto Route 806 (Martz Road) and go about 1/2 mile. Cemetery is on small hill on right side of road beside large poultry house. Property is owned by Andrew Yancey.
NotesCemetery is enclosed with a fence. Notes in the Remarks Column were written by J.W. Wayland 1926 and 1930.
Survey Date and Recorder01 Aug 2008
Deb Wake & Gene Holsinger
