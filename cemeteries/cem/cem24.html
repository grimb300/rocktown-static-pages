Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryGreenwood Family Cemetery (*)
LocationRunkles Gap, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Harrisonburg, take Route 33 East to Route 602 and turn left, then left at the residence of Gene Good, 4945 East Point Road, who is the owner of the property on which the cemetery is located.
NotesRow 6 - These graves are buried near the brick wall. Row 7 - There is a small section that is enclosed by a brick wall and this is the original portion of the cemetery and has a plaque erected that reads: Wiilliam Edward Gaines 1798 - 1875; Elizabeth Shotwell Gaines 1803 - 1884; Erected by Edward Christian Gaines - 1935. * Records show "this is a colored cemetery".
Survey Date and RecorderNov 2001
Sue Propst
