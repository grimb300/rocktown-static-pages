Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryWhitmore Family Cemetery
LocationKeezletown, Rockingham County, Virginia. Located on the original Whitmore farm and is presently owned by the Brunk family. The cemetery is at the base of Massanutten Mountain just below Laird's Knob and about one and one-half miles Southeast of the intersection of Route 620 and Route 724 at Stonegate, Northeast of Keezletown. The cemetery is difficult to locate as it is isolated in a wooded area. The approximate coordinates are 38 - 26 - 55 N Lat. and 78 - 45 - 15 W Lon.
NotesThere are several unmarked stones of which one is believed to belong to Benjamin Whitmore, born 1795 and died July 10, 1855.
