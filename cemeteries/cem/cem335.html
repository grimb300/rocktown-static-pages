Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryFlick Family Cemetery 1
LocationLinville, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Harrisonburg, Virginia, take Route 753 (Kratzer Road) towards the community of Linville. Cemetery was located behind stone house on right side of the road before entering Linville.
NotesCemetery is no longer in existence. In 1967 J. Robert Swank recorded the headstones and noted: "This plot was approximately 30 x 60 feet - space for 30 to 45 graves on the Flick farm. In an open field south east of barn. Death records in Clerks office at County Courthouse shows: Benjamin Flick d. 5 Nov 1889 aged 67 yrs son of Henry; John H. Flick died 11 Apr 1883 aged 63 yrs son of Joseph & Eliza; Sallie Flick died 27 Dec 1888 aged 5 yrs dau. Of Abram & Ida." In 1885 the farm was owned by Benjamin Flick and 2006 owned by Mr. Depoy.
Survey Date and Recorder2 Feb 2006
Bob & Lois Emswiler
