CemeteryPleasant Valley Mennonite Fellowship Church Cem.
LocationPleasant Valley area, Rockingham County, Virginia. From the corner of Route 11 south (Main Street) and Route 659 (Port Republic Road) travel east 5.3 miles on Route 659. Turn right onto Route 276 (Cross Keys Road). Go a short distance and turn right onto Route 679 (Battlefield Road). Go to Route 681 (N Whitesel Church Road) and turn left. Cemetery is on the left side of the road.
NotesThis cemetery is adjacent to Whitesel United Brethren Church Cemetery. Well maintained with chain around it.
