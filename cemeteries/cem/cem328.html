Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryCoffman Farm Cemetery
LocationRockingham County, Virginia. From Harrisonburg, Virginia, take Route 753 (Kratzer Road) north towards Linville. Go approximately 3 miles and cemetery is located at 2985 Kratzer Road, on the left side of road behind the Coffman house.
NotesThe plot is unfenced and in poor condition. Stones are broken and off bases. In 1967 J. Robert Swank made the following recordings taken from County records. BIRTHS-- Ameila Rutherford Born 28 Apr 1872 on Kratzer Road, dau of Thomas H & Sallie; Emma E. Rutherford born 12 Nov 1878, dau of Gerard & Alice C.; John P. Rutherford born 17 Jan 1880 son of Gerard & Alice C. DEATHS-- G. C. Rutherford died 18 Aug 1883 aged 3y 4m 25d son of Wm. & Sarah; George W. Rutherford died 9 Mar 1872 aged 24y son of Wm. H. & Elizabeth; John P. Rutherford died 3 Jun 1881 aged 1y 5m son of G. C. & Mary A.; W. H. Rutherford died 5 Oct 1890 aged 72 yrs; Wm. H. Rutherford died 27 Oct 1889 aged 72 yrs son of Thos. & Eliz.
Survey Date and Recorder6 Nov 2005
Bob & Lois Emswiler; Robert Coffman
