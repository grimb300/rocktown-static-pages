Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryLewis Ritchie Cemetery
LocationFulks Run area, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Fulks Run Grocery store, go west on Hwy 259 for 1 mile. At Valley Land store, turn left onto Route 818 (Little Dry River Road). Watch for Route 631. About 1 mile after you pass Route 631, the road from Carr Hollow will enter Dry River Road from the right. Around the next turn is the old Ritchie land. The cemetery is on the left side of the road on the hill next to the road.
NotesThe hillside, where the cemetery is located, is covered with perIwinkle. There are no stones marking the site. For more details on the Ritchie family refer to book "Isaac Ritchie of Virginia" by Vergie Carr Lantz. Directions and notes were furnished by Patricia Turner Ritchie in 1999. In 1967 J. Robert Swank noted "space would indicate 35 to 50 graves. No inscribed stones."
