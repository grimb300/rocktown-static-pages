Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryThomas Family Cemetery 2
LocationHarrisonburg, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Harrisonburg, Virginia, take Route 42 south. Turn left onto Route 726 (Erickson Avenue). Cross railroad tracks and turn right on Pear Street. Go 5/10ths mile and cemetery is located on the right (south) side of Pear Street.
NotesLurty Landes provided cemetery burial information in the 1960's. He was the owner of the land at that time and was a great, great grandson of John and Susanna Thomas. Cemetery restored by Margaret Tysinger, Bud Tysinger, Carolyn & Don Landis, and Desmon Wichael. Additional family members contributed to the purchase of the Thomas Family stone. The stones of John & Susanna Thomas were badly broken, just a little stack of stones. They were pieced together and placed them in concrete along with other broken stones with no names. A new stone was placed marking the cemetery.
Survey Date and RecorderJan 2007
Margaret Tysinger & others
