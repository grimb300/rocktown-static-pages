CemeterySt. Lukes United Church of Christ Cemetery
LocationCootes Store area, Rockingham County, Virginia. Located on county line between Rockingham and Shenandoah Counties. From Court Square, Harrisonburg, Virginia, take Route 42 north (Harpine Hwy) 12.1 miles to Broadway. Turn left onto Route 259 (Brocks Gap Road) and go 3.3 to Route 613 (North Mountain Road) and turn right. Go 6.1 miles and church/cemetery on the left side of road.
NotesCemetery is well maintained. In 1967 J. Robert Swank note “Indications of many unmarked graves. Cemetery rows are recorded from street to the church (east to west) and headstones from left to right (south to north).
