CemeteryBethesda Mennonite Church Cemetery
LocationBroadway area, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Court Square in Harrisonburg, Virginia take Route 42 North (Harpine Highway) 12.1 miles to Broadway. In Broadway turn left onto Route 259 (Brocks Gap Road) and go 3.3 miles to Route 613 (North Mountain Road) and turn right. Go 1.3 miles and church/cemetery is on the left side of the road.
NotesCemetery established since previous records in 1960 and well maintained.
