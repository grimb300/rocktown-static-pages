Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryFink Cemetery
LocationBergton Area, Rockingham County, Virginia. Located on the Shenandoah Mountain west of Bergton, Virginia. On the farm of the late John & Barbara Dove Fink, later to become the William Wade Turner farm.
NotesCemetery records compiled by Shirley Cullers Miller using headstones, field/river rocks, papers of Warren Souder and Lewis Yankey, J. Robert Swank’s 1967 records, and through interviews with area residents. Shirley Cullers Miller noted: Cemetery was referred to as the Thomas Cemetery, but local residents have always called it the Fink Cemetery. In 1967 J. Robert Swank referred to it as the Siever Cemetery and that it was fenced and in good condition.
Survey Date and Recorder8 Dec 2002
Shirley Cullers Miller
