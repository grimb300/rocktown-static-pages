Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryBowman Family Cemetery 1
LocationMauzy Area, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Harrisonburg, Virginia, take Route 11 north (North Valley Pike) to Route 608 (Mauzy Athlone Road) and turn right. Travel about 1/2 mile on Route 608. Cemetery is in field on right side of Route 608. In 1960 Walter J. Sellers owned this property.
NotesIn 2011 no attempt was made to locate this cemetery. In 1960s D.A.R. noted the 3 Rhodes burials were moved to E.U.B. Church Cemetery in Lacey Springs, Va. about 50 years ago. Note-The E.U.B. Church is gone but was located near the Lacey Spring Cemetery.
Survey Date and Recorder1960s
