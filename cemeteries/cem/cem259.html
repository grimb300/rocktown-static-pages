CemeteryPort Republic Cemetery
LocationPort Republic, Rockingham County, Virginia. In Harrisonburg, from the corner of Main Street (Route 11) and Port Republic Road (Route 659), travel east 11.1 miles on Port Republic Road. Turn right onto Route 605 (Main Street). Route 605 turns left and Route 1606 (Kemper Street) goes straight ahead. Go straight on Route 1606 and you will see cemetery on the left side of the road. It is 1.0 miles from Corner of Routes 659 and 605.
