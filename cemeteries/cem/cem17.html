Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryHuffman Family Cemetery
LocationTenth Legion, Rockingham County, Virginia. East of Tenth Legion off Route 620 near Arkton on the Lawrence Reid farm. It is on a hill West of Mr. Reid's entrance from Route 620.
NotesThis cemetery was recorded in 1979 by Mary Marie Koontz Arrington and published in her book "Mountain Valley People" in 1982. Note by Ms. Arrington - The large limestone slab could be Abraham Huffman's grave. He married Catherine Bolton on 18 September 1836 in Augusta County, Virginia.
