Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryBear Lithia Spring Cemetery
LocationElkton area, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Elkton, Virginia, go north on Route 340 (East Side Highway) to Route 607 (Bear Lithia Lane) and turn left.
NotesIn 2011 no attempt was made to locate this cemetery. This cemetery has been documented by numerous sources, which have conflicting recordings. Some of them are: the WPA Record of 1936; Daily News Record Harrisonburg, Virginia, article 18 Nov 1966; Harrisonburg-Rockingham Historical Society Newsletter Vol. I No. 3 Winter 1979; and the 1937 record of John W. Wayland, 26 WFCHS, Stewart Bell Jr. Archives, Handley Regional Library, Winchester, Virginia. Mr. Wayland noted-the old graveyard is out in a field, unfenced. Two or three other graves are marked with rough stones. Doubtless a number of persons are buried there whose graves are not marked. The plat is about 200 yards (maybe 150) SW of the great spring.
Survey Date and Recorder1960s
