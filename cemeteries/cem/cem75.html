Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryOffenbacker Family Cemetery
LocationNorth of Elkton, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Harrisonburg go east on Route 33 to Elkton. In Elkton go left (north) onto Route 340 (East Side Hwy). Turn right onto Route 607 (Red Brush Road) to Route 603 (Blose Road) and go left. Cemetery is on the right in the back of Mac J. Offenbacker's place, through a marshy field. This is private property.
NotesCemetery is overgrown, especially around edges. There are several metal markers with no inscription and small graves which are unmarked. Comments in the "Remarks" column were provided by Mac J. Offenbacker and from the DAR records of 1960s.
Survey Date and Recorder27 Sep 1987
Lois Bowman
