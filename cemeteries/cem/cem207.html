Rockingham County Virginia Tombstones by Cemetery

CemeteryJacob Sellers Cemetery
LocationMauzy area, Rockingham County, Virginia. From Harrisonburg, Virginia, take Route 11 North to Mauzy and cross over I-81, then take a right onto Route 608 (Mauzy Athlone Road). Continue on Route 608 and take a right onto Route 717 (Indian Trail Road). Cemetery is next to the road on the right side.
NotesCemetery is fenced and well maintained. There is a marker over the gate entrance that reads "Rife & Crist, Timberville, Va. 1894" which we believe was the company that installed the fence around the cemetery.
Survey Date and Recorder17 Apr 2003
Bennie Getz, Walter Mason, Bob & Lois Emswiler
